/$ instructions

begin by inputting the command ‘init’ in the terminal section below. players will take turns typing in their turns into the terminal. Use commands such as ‘top_left’ & ‘middle’ to signify your turn. a small graphical hint to the right of the screen will help show previous turns.

click the hint button towards the top right of the screen for all commands & other hints

/? commands

  • 'init'

    - begins initial game

  • 'clear'

    - starts a new game

  • 'top_left'

    - move top left of board

  • 'top_centre'

    - move top centre of board

  • 'top_right'

    - move top right of board

  • 'middle_left'

    - move middle left of board

  • 'middle'

    - move middle of board

  • 'middle_right'

    - move middle right of board

  • 'bottom_left'

    - move bottom left of board

  • 'bottom_centre'

    - move bottom centre of board

  • 'bottom_right'

    - move bottom right of board
