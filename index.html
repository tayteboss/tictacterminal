/$ instructions

initiate the game by inputting the command ‘init’ in the terminal section below. players will take turns typing in their move into the terminal. use commands such as ‘top_left’ & ‘middle’ to signify your move. a small graphical hint to the right of the screen will help show the current board. first player to win 2 rounds wins the game.

/? commands

  • 'init' - begins initial game

  • 'clear' - starts a new game

  • 'top_left' - move top left of board

  • 'top_centre' - move top centre of board

  • 'top_right' - move top right of board

  • 'middle_left' - move middle left of board

  • 'middle' - move middle of board

  • 'middle_right' - move middle right of board

  • 'bottom_left' - move bottom left of board

  • 'bottom_centre' - move bottom centre of board

  • 'bottom_right' - move bottom right of board
